Welcome to Andy's Trivia App

   
       

About the App

       

Andy's Trivia App is a fun and interactive platform to test your knowledge with custom trivia cards. Create, manage, and play with your own sets of questions, or challenge yourself with sets created by others (admin access required).

   
   
       

About the Builder

       

Built by Andy, a passionate developer who loves creating engaging web applications. This app was crafted to bring the joy of trivia to everyone, with a focus on simplicity and user-friendly design.

   
   
       

Purpose

       

The purpose of Andy's Trivia App is to provide a platform for learning and entertainment through trivia. Whether you're studying for a test or just love trivia, you can create personalized card sets and play at your own pace.
